About Us

You have an idea. We help you build it into a business.

Starting or growing a business is hard — especially when you're doing it alone. At the Renasant Center For Ideas, we sit down with entrepreneurs right here in Tupelo and help you cut through the noise: funding, business planning, licensing, marketing — whatever's standing between you and moving forward.

We specialize in supporting women-owned and minority-owned businesses, and we understand the specific landscape of Northeast Mississippi. Whether you're launching something new, looking to expand, or thinking about relocating to Lee County, we know who to call and what programs are actually available to you.

"There's more support available to small business owners in this region than most people realize. We're here to connect you to it."
